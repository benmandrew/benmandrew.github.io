Hello!

I'm a PhD researcher at the University of Manchester, investigating ways to mathematically prove the safety and reliability of robots. I'm supervised by Marie Farrell, Louise Dennis, and Michael Fisher, working in the Autonomy and Verification Group.

Previously I worked at Tarides, developing open-source tooling for the OCaml ecosystem.

I enjoy making computer science more accessible, whether by writing articles, making fun interactive projects, or just rambling to passers-by. You can check out my blog here.
